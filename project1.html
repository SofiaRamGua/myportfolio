Dreamcatcher Iteration

This project consisted of interpreting a dreamcatcher in various ways using different art styles and media. I was allowed to do a lot of experimentation with media and concepts that could help me represent my object. For my final pieces, i focused on the idea of dreaming so i opted to create surreal compositions to signify the purpose of the dreamcatcher. For the materials, i used paper cut outs, drawings and paint so create interesting artwork.

What I learned.
This project taught me how to think outside of the box and how to combine different medias.
