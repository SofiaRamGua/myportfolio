Seasonal palettes

Objective

The goal for this project was to create a poster to show the colors of the seasons for a location of our choice along with a description that is relavant for the color palettes that were created.

My idea

I chose the country Brazil because of my interest about the culture and language of this country. I was intrigued by the colorful nature of Brazil and wondered how even the coldest seasons of the year are represented by vibrant colors. I wanted the design on my poster to be fun and excited to fit the atmosphere of Brazil.

Process

I began this project by researching photos of Brazil during each season which would allow me to get an idea of the palettes. From the photos, I used the color drop tool to help me identify each color. I created multiple palettes to chose from. I continued this project by researching important events, activities, and landmarks that happen during the seasons. From there, I eliminated colors until I concluded a palatte that best represented the colorful essence of Brazil for each season.
