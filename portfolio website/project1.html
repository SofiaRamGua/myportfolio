Dreamcatcher Iteration

Objective

The objective of this project was to select an object to then repeat it in various compositons with the use of unlimited medias and concepts.

My idea

I chose a dreamcatcher as my object because it's an interesting object that has a lot of meaning and history. My idea was to research the dreamcatcher and use the information gathered to create my visuals. I wanted to make these conceptual ideas visible through my compositons.

Process

After I concluded my research, I began with dissecting parts of the dreamcatcher and repeating them in paint, marker, charcoal, ink, crayons, etc,. I experimented with this media until I was satisfied. I then moved to experimenting with types of backgrounds like different types of paper, and canvas. I combined these compositions until I achieved the most meaningful pieces. When the pieces were done, I transfer them to my computer where I manipulated them to make them even better. Using photoshop and Illustrator I was able to incorporate photography and typography along with other ideas that surged through the process.
